### ğŸ§© What Are Spatio-Temporal Foundation Models (STFMs)?
**Spatio-Temporal Foundation Models (STFMs)** are large, pre-trained AI models built to understand and predict data that evolves across both **time** and **space**.  

They combine three key abilities:
1. **Perception** â€” recognizing patterns and correlations in data.  
2. **Optimization** â€” improving systems such as transportation and climate forecasting.  
3. **Reasoning** â€” making logical, explainable decisions from complex data.  

---

### âš™ï¸ Why They Matter
Traditional deep learning models usually focus on one task at a time and need lots of labeled data.  
**STFMs**, inspired by **large language models (LLMs)**, can:
- Adapt to new tasks with minimal training.  
- Learn from multiple data types (text, maps, sensors, videos).  
- Enable intelligent decision-making in real-world systems like smart cities, weather prediction, and disaster management.
